Design Florida's neighborhood-level resilience plan.

TideTurner helps students, residents, and local leaders model coastal actions and instantly see tradeoffs for flooding risk, heat, insurance burden, and habitat recovery.

Florida problem, clearly framed

Many communities face the same dangerous loop: high-tide flooding damages roads, poor drainage causes contamination after storms, and marine habitat loss makes surge impacts worse. TideTurner turns this into an interactive decision game grounded in local priorities.

What makes this novel

  • Neighborhood scenario composer instead of static info page.
  • Interactive budget allocator with visible consequences.
  • Livability score blended from safety, cost, ecology, and equity.
